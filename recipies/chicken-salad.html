Easy Chicken Sald

Description

This oh so yummy recipie will have you wanting seconds of these amazing layered nachos. With all the toppings you desire this recipie will knock your socks off.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded cheese
  • Jalapenos
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Brown the Ground beef and mix with the Seasoning
  2. Chop all of the veggies for the toppings
  3. Put Tortilla Chips on baking sheet, add meat and cheese. Put in oven for 3 mins or until cheese is melted.
  4. Remove from oven and add all desired toppings.
