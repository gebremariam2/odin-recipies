Lasagna Recipe

Description

This amazing lasagna recipe features a hearty, well layered masterpiece of a meal. From the savory meat sauce to the creamy cheesy layers, this is a meal you won't want to miss!.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta sauce
  • Italian Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce (I love this brand) & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
